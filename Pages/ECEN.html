Edinburgh Circular Economy Network

Concept

The Edinburgh Circular Economy Network aims to support circular economy initiatives in and around Edinburgh.

As there was no specific creative guidance for how this logo should look, I took inspiration from the natural beauty of the iconic Edinburgh landmark, Arthur's Seat.

Paired with the distinctive city skyline, I challenged myself to simplify this quintessentially Edinburgh view into a logo which celebrates the city's green spaces.

Development

I approached this by layering and refining basic shapes, as I would if working with paper. Using a reference image, I experimented with different colour blocking methods.

I pared down the colours I was using to express definition and lighting, although I chose a wider-than-usual palette to express the green tones catching the light. This felt fitting for an environmentally-focused organisation like ECEN.

Final design

The finished logo, where text and a more structured layout has been added. To account for both dark and light backgrounds, slight tweaks to the green colour were made for better contrast.

Lastly, I created a simple favicon to mirror the style of the main logo.
