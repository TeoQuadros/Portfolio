Footprints Africa

Concept

Designed by MUTI

Footprints Africa is a non-profit organisation working to advance sustainable development of local African economies.

I created this vibrant set of illustrations to replace stock photos in the card elements, to give the page a more cohesive feel. The colour palette and visual style are inspired by this header image for the page, by MUTI design studio (left).

Development

I sampled my colour palette form the existing MUTI illustration, aiming to keep this cohesive with MUTI's image.
Using the same shadow and highlight colours really helped to inform the way I defined shape in my own graphics.
