My Skills (at a glance)

The Human

I’m an open minded, offbeat person. I delight in the weird and wonderful of the everyday. I’m an enjoyer of street art and pigeons, drinker of large cappuccinos and aficionado of Grotesque fonts. I love swimming in cold lakes, eating warm croissants and late-night charcoal sketching sessions. I’m a big fan of those people who hook mushrooms up to electrodes and produce ambient-electronic mushroom music.

The Creative

I have been painting and drawing since I was capable of holding a paintbrush. My lifelong love of art has led me to design, as I feel like it’s a really tangible way to bring more visual interest into the everyday. I take great joy in solving design challenges, and I’m fascinated by the intersection of core design principles and technology.
