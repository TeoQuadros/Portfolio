Hello, I'm Teo (he/they)

I’m an open minded, offbeat kind of person who delights in the weird and wonderful of the everyday. I’m an aficionado of Grotesque fonts and a big fan of those people who hook mushrooms up to electrodes and produce ambient-electronic mushroom music. When not designing, I’m most likely holed up in my apartment cooking Japanese comfort food and charcoal sketching into the wee hours. When I do go outside, you might find me wandering around my home city of Poznań - cappuccino in hand - enjoying the street art and photographing the local pigeons or, occasionally, donning my running shoes to hurtle down the side of a mountain.

starving artist

hungry designer

As an artist who struggled to reconcile my need to create with the realities of capitalism, I fell into the design world, then realising its potential as a wonderful means of having more art in our lives. Over 4 years I have gained experience as an all-round designer with HappyPorch and, while our partnership is ongoing, I’m looking for a new adventure.

I’m the kind of designer who doodles their concepts on paper, and treats every project like an adventure. In a world of digital overwhelm, I hope to offer a design experience that feels bespoke, connective and human. If you are looking for a designer who focuses on your intent and identity, then we might be a great match.
