African Stories

Project Type: Web Graphics

Footprints Africa is a non-profit organisation that’s on a mission to advance sustainable, scalable and inclusive approaches to development of local African economies.

 

Footprints has a nice collection of African circular economy case studies. We thought that this project could take those case studies to the next level by building an interactive online stories platform where users can virtually experience the stories by taking a journey across Africa.
